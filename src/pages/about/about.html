About About

MANSOC is an accredited organization for Business Administration Students of Holy Angel University.

For thirty in existence, it has been providing avenues not only in academics but as well as experiences in worthwhile activities with exposure to actual business and professional experiences.

This organization is a affiliated with the Philippine Junior Marketing Association (PJMA), a national student organization and a youth affiliate of Philippine Marketing Association (PMA) which holds seminars, trainings, conventions, and other opportunities for future business leaders.
